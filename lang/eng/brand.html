What is a filler?

Filler (dermal filler) is an injection form medical device that injects materials that are safe to the human body, into the dermis layer of the face to remove wrinkles and replenish skin tissue to add in volume.

Benefits of filler

  • Treatment not a surgical procedure, therefore enhances beauty naturally.
  • It is a simple treatment which usually takes no more than five to ten minutes.
  • Leaves less post-treatment scarring, thus doesn’t require extra recovery period.
  • Shows immediate volume up effects
